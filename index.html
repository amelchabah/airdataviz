About the work

Dataviz, a student project

This data visualization represents the evolution of international tourism over the 2010s, till today. As you scroll, discover the numbers of tourism through interactive and animated designs. This short project was made by three multimedia students on their second year of bachelor. Scroll and enjoy your trip!

Before & After Covid19

The tourism industry has collapsed with the COVID-19 pandemic, border closures and travel restrictions.
